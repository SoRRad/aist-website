# News images

Place news images here. Filenames should match the `image` field in each
news item's entry in `lib/news.ts`.

Recommended dimensions: 1600×900 (16:9 aspect), optimized JPEG, under 200KB.

If an image is missing, the news card will gracefully fall back to a
styled gradient placeholder showing the category and date.

## Current news items expecting images

- `madani-visit-2025.jpg` — Dr. Amin Madani visit to Mayo Clinic
- `sages-2026.jpg` — SAGES Annual Meeting 2026
- `asmbs-2026.jpg` — ASMBS Annual Meeting 2026

## Optimization tip

Use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) or similar to compress images before
committing. A 1600×900 photo should target ~80–150KB as JPEG at quality 80.
